# KindEarth Social Policy Analyser & Forecasting Tool

## Purpose
Detect policy drift, stress accumulation, and curvature over time.

## Status
Planned / under specification.

## Core signals (draft)
- Drift (baseline erosion)
- Contradiction accumulation across interfaces
- Syntropy vs entropy trajectories
- Scenario exploration (not prediction)

## Non-goals
- Predicting outcomes
- Scoring individuals
- Enabling coercion or compliance enforcement
